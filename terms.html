1. Introduction

Welcome to DND Global Logistics Limited ("DND"). These Terms and Conditions govern your access to our website, digital platforms, and logistics services including courier, haulage, freight forwarding, warehousing, and vehicle hire.

By engaging our services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to be bound by these Terms.

2. Service Requests & Fees

All service requests are subject to acceptance by DND. We reserve the right to refuse any shipment. Rates provided are based on weight, dimensions, and destination. Any variation in declared details may result in price adjustments.

3. Prohibited Items

Customers are strictly prohibited from shipping:

  • Illegal drugs or narcotics
  • Explosives, firearms, or ammunition
  • Perishable goods (unless specially arranged)
  • Live animals
  • Items prohibited by Nigerian Law or NDLEA regulations

4. Liability & Insurance

Our liability for loss or damage is limited to the declared value of the shipment at the time of booking, subject to our maximum liability caps. We recommend "Extended Liability" coverage for high-value items.

5. Governing Law

These terms are governed by the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. Disputes shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of Nigerian courts.

  • Personal ID (Name, Email, Phone Number)

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Act, you have the right to access your data, request corrections, or ask for the deletion of your personal records from our system.
